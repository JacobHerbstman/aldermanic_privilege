\section{Additional Density RD Checks}
\label{sec:appendix_density}

This appendix reports covariate balance and the two density RD checks retained for the shorter draft.
The shifted-cutoff checks ask whether similar discontinuities appear at fake boundaries nearby.
The donut checks ask whether the main pattern depends only on parcels immediately adjacent to the ward boundary.

\subsection{Covariate Balance}

Table~\ref{tab:density_covariate_balance} compares observed neighborhood characteristics across the ward boundaries used in the density analysis.
The multifamily sample is broadly balanced, while balance is weaker in the all-construction sample, one reason I focus on multifamily projects and treat the all-construction estimates as supporting evidence.

\subsection{Donut RD Checks}

Figures~\ref{fig:rd_donut_25ft} and~\ref{fig:rd_donut_50ft} exclude parcels within 25ft or 50ft of the ward boundary in the 500ft samples.
The fitted discontinuity stays negative in both outcomes, so the main pattern is not coming only from parcels right at the cutoff.
