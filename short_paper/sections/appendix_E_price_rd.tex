\section{Additional Price Boundary Checks}
\label{sec:appendix_price_rd}

This appendix reports placebo-cutoff checks for the rent and home-sales boundary comparisons in Section~\ref{sec:empirical_results_prices}.
The figures move the cutoff 1000ft inside each side of the true ward boundary and re-estimate the same 500ft boundary comparison around those fake cutoffs.
The placebo windows do not overlap the main true-boundary 500ft sample.

\begin{figure}[H]
    \centering
    \includegraphics[width=0.92\textwidth]{../tasks/price_rd_stress_tests/output/rent_placebo_rd_main_style.pdf}
    \caption{Listed-Rent Placebo Cutoffs}
    \figurenotes{Figure repeats the listed-rent boundary comparison at placebo cutoffs shifted 1000ft inside each side of the true ward boundary.
    The sample applies the clean-location restriction defined in Appendix~\ref{subsec:renthub_sample} and includes studios.
    The left panel shifts the cutoff into the lenient side, the center panel shows the true boundary, and the right panel shifts the cutoff into the stringent side.
    Distances are oriented so that positive values point toward the more stringent side of the corresponding true ward boundary.
    Dots are binned means of log listed rent in 2022 dollars after removing segment-by-month fixed effects, hedonic controls, and amenity controls.
    Panel subtitles report the corresponding placebo estimates from the same flat 500ft specification, with standard errors clustered by boundary segment.}
    \label{fig:rent_placebo_boundaries}
\end{figure}
