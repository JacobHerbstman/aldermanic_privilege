\section{Additional Rental and Home Sales Results}
\label{sec:appendix_sales_rental}

This appendix collects additional rental-market and home-sales evidence paralleling the slide appendix.

\subsection{Permutation Test: Rental Border Fixed Effects}

Figure~\ref{fig:rental_permutation_appendix} reports a permutation test for the rental border fixed-effect specification. The observed estimate lies in the extreme left tail of the permutation distribution, indicating that the rental result is unlikely to be generated by arbitrary reassignment of treatment across ward pairs.

\end{figure}

\subsection{Event Study Without Hedonic Controls}

Figure~\ref{fig:event_study_sales_no_hedonics} reports a complementary home-sales event study estimated without property-level hedonic controls. This specification is useful for checking whether the positive post-redistricting price path is purely a compositional artifact. The qualitative pattern remains similar, suggesting that the home-sales result is not driven entirely by changes in the mix of transacted properties.
