\section{Additional Event-Study Evidence}
\label{sec:appendix_event_study}

This appendix reports treated-versus-control balance tables for the two redistricting designs used in the paper. In both cases, treatment is assigned at the census-block level, so the balance checks are reported at that level as well. The covariates here are block-level averages of exact parcel-based amenity distances and zoned FAR, constructed from the underlying parcel geography rather than block-group ACS aggregates.
