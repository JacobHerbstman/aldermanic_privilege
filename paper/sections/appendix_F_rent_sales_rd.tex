\section{Additional Rent and Home-Sales Border RD Checks}
\label{sec:appendix_rent_sales_rd}

This appendix reports cross-sectional border RD checks for rents and home sales. These are not the redistricting event-study specifications in Sections~\ref{sec:empirical_results_permits} and~\ref{sec:empirical_results_home_sales}. Instead, they ask whether prices differ across ward boundaries in the current local-border design after adding the same external amenity controls used in the updated balance tables. Park distance is measured to official Chicago Park District park-boundary polygons.
