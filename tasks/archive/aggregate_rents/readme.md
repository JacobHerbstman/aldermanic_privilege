# aggregate_rents

Purpose: Builds internal aggregate rent diagnostics with citywide YoY plots and ward/community-area growth maps for RentHub raw, a cleaned citywide RentHub variant, and Dwellsy.

Produces: `output/rent_geography_monthly_summary.csv`, `output/rent_geography_yearly_summary.csv`, `output/rent_map_period_summary.csv`, `output/rent_coverage_summary.csv`, `output/rent_citywide_yoy_monthly.csv`, `output/rent_citywide_yoy_longrun.pdf`, `output/rent_citywide_yoy_recent.pdf`, and rent growth maps for wards and community areas.

Approx. runtime: ~5-25 minutes.
