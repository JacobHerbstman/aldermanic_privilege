# aggregate_home_sales

Purpose: Builds internal aggregate home-sales diagnostics with citywide YoY plots and ward/community-area growth maps from the geocoded sales panel.

Produces: `output/home_sales_geography_monthly_summary.csv`, `output/home_sales_geography_yearly_summary.csv`, `output/home_sales_map_period_summary.csv`, `output/home_sales_coverage_summary.csv`, `output/home_sales_citywide_yoy_monthly.csv`, `output/home_sales_citywide_yoy_annual.csv`, `output/home_sales_citywide_yoy_annual_longrun.pdf`, `output/home_sales_citywide_yoy_recent_monthly.pdf`, `output/home_sales_citywide_transaction_yoy_recent_monthly.pdf`, and home-sales growth maps for wards and community areas.

Approx. runtime: ~1-5 minutes.
